    Life around the Loft

    Our Story

    Tucked into a quiet corner of the French Alps, Logan’s Loft began as a far-fetched ambition for a family of educators from Huntington Beach, California. We spent our lives teaching, and eventually, we learned that the best lessons come from a change of scenery. Swapping the Pacific surf for the Grand Massif peaks was a dream we didn’t just want to keep—we wanted to share it.

    If you value a well-loved kitchen, a view that stops you in your tracks, and the kind of profound quiet that only altitude can provide, you’ll feel at home here. We haven’t chased luxury; we’ve chased warmth and honesty.

    The loft accommodates 10 with ease (eight in beds, two on the futon), making it a sanctuary for the whole crew. It’s a place designed for post-mountain recovery: gather at the oversized dining table for a long raclette dinner, or retreat to the balcony as the peaks turn purple in the twilight.
