LOGAN’S LOFT

Morillon, French Alps — Grand Massif

The Place

Logan’s Loft is a practical alpine basecamp.
Built for families who want to spend time in the mountains — not pass through.

Not a polished resort stay.
A place to live for a bit. Ski. Walk. Cook. Slow down.

Why It Works

  • Steps to the Morillon gondola
  • Sleeps 10
  • Walkable to Lac Bleu, Giffre River, shops
  • Private parking
  • Full kitchen

The Experience

Mornings start slow. Coffee. Light over the mountains.

Winter → straight to the lift.
Summer → lake, trails, bikes.

Kids stay busy. Adults get space back.
Simple. In a good way.

Location

Morillon sits in the Grand Massif with access to Morillon, Samoëns, Les Carroz, and Flaine.
Less crowded. Same terrain.
