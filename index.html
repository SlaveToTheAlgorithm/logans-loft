    Life around it

    Our Story

    Tucked in the French Alps, right next to the gondola in Morillon, this place started as a crazy idea—and somehow turned into something real. We’re from Huntington Beach, California, and come from a family of educators. Owning a holiday home in France? That’s a dream come true. But a place like this isn’t much good unless we can share it.

    If you're anything like us—people who love good meals, big views, fresh air, and the kind of quiet you can’t get in a city—then you’ll get it. We’ve kept it simple, grounded, and warm—just the way we like it.

    The apartment sleeps up to 10: eight in beds and two on the futon. It’s big enough for your whole crew—family, friends, or a mix of both. It’s not fancy, but it’s honest. Comfortable. Easy to settle into. The kind of place you come back to.

    After a day out, come back and soak in the whirlpool tub (it really is heaven), gather around the big dining table for a home-cooked meal, or kick back on the balcony as the sun drops behind the peaks.
