The Space

Steps from the Slopes

Located on the third floor in the heart of Morillon village. In winter, you are steps from the gondola and can ski down the blue run directly back to the building entrance. In summer, the alpine trails and Lac Bleu are your front yard.

The Summer Local’s Guide

    Our Story

    Logan’s Loft began as a far-fetched ambition for a family of educators from Huntington Beach, California. We’ve spent our lives in classrooms, and eventually, we learned that the best lessons come from a change of scenery. Swapping the Pacific surf for the Grand Massif peaks was a dream we wanted to share.

    This isn't a corporate rental; it's a beloved vacation home under new ownership that honors the charming alpine experience guests have loved for over a decade. Whether you're here for the whirlpool tub, the big dining table, or the sunset views from the west-facing balcony—you're welcome here.
